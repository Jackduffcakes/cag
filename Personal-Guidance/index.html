Personal Guidance

Everyone has something unique to offer. The general tips only get you so far and are more helpful for understanding some of the hidden nuances, but only you know what your strongest or most desirable trait is. Determining what that is (and it isn’t limited to just one thing) and showcasing it is crucial to constructing a cohesive and compelling college application.
