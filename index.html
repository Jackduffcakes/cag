Partnership with WriteKey in the works

How Is This Site Different?

In 2018, the majority of applicants to any particular college have similar grades, scores, and often extracurriculars. How do admissions officers choose?

Admissions officers look beyond grades and scores to the "little things," the intangibles. Applicants are differentiated through traits like intellectual curiosity and intrinsic motivation.

College Admission Guidance covers these intangibles, provides a guide for each grade in high school, an explanation of the college process, application strategy, testing help, and recommendations of outside resources.
