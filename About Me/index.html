About Me

My name is Jack Duffy. As I stated earlier this website is really just my analysis of the process and me doing the research that I don’t believe people should have to do.

I realized only recently that the way I approached the process wasn’t as common as I thought; I realized then that all the time I spent analyzing and understanding these nuances and intangibles could be of so much more value in the hands of others helping them get into college.

I am an 18 year old graduating high school senior in the PA area. Entering Dartmouth come fall I am unclear on my major, but I do know that I love strategy and entrepreneurship is interesting to me. My parents both went to a fairly average college in Iowa and my brother graduated from Cornell University in 2017.

I say all this only to establish that I am not extraordinary. I just understand the admission process really well.

I love to help people and so feel free to contact me.

If people are interested I can give my personal stats for the application process (grades, test scores, etc), but really that won’t be too helpful for any of you.

If you like the content I provide donating is very helpful, for even though I am not doing this for money, I can’t be spending tons of time on here if I am making no money at all.

Thank you for checking out my content and for your interest in me.




Donating is not required or expected; it allows me to continue putting a lot of work into the website, though, and help those going through the college process.
